About The Band

Psychedelic Flies emerged from the underground music scene in 2018, blending psychedelic rock with electronic influences to create a unique sonic experience that transports listeners to new dimensions.

The band consists of four passionate musicians who share a vision of pushing the boundaries of contemporary music while paying homage to the psychedelic pioneers of the past.

With mesmerizing live performances and thought-provoking lyrics, Psychedelic Flies continues to build a dedicated following around the world.
